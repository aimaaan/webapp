IIUM Sejahtera Global Mission: Eid al-Adha @ Bumi Champa, Journey of Khalifah to Cambodia 2023

Masjid Sultan Hj. Ahmad Shah (SHAS) management, in collaboration with Office of the Campus Director IIUM Kuantan and the Highest Council for Islamic Religious Affairs Cambodia (HICIRAC), would continue to organise our annual Qurban/Aqiqah activity and CSR for the coming Eid al-Adha 1444H/2023 at Kompong Chhnang Province, Cambodia.

Amongst the scheduled activities are:

a) Qurban / Aqiqah activity during Eid al-Adha & distribution of meat portions to the poor,
b) Religious talk by IIUM to the Muslim communities,
c) Distribution of donated Water Pumps and
d) Distribution of donated goods (i.e telekung, sajadah, robes, tasbih etc) to school children and local community/village

Therefore, we would like to extend this invitation to the IIUM Community and the general public to take part in the Qurban / Aqiqah portion, extending donation of Water Pumps, goods and cash in order to support our fellow Muslims in Cambodia. Kindly share and extend this invitation to your family members, friends, colleagues, neighbours, qariah Masjid, private and government agencies etc as well. Kindly contact our secretariat for further details and enquiries. We pray to Allah SWT to guide, bless and bestow us with His blessing and pleasure (ridhwan Allah) in this world and hereafter for our kind support towards this program.
